Ocean's Six

Ocean's 6 created different maps to find eddies and contoured map and diagrams. They also created an algorithm that identifies eddies. This group got their data from Copernicus marine. They didn't have to clean their data because it was already arranged in a Numpy array. Also created data visualizations.

Languages & Libraries

  • The language that was used for this project was Python.
  • The libraries used are Matplotlib, numpy, netcdf4, and daytime.

Challenges:

  • A difficulty was plotting, the x, y, z-axes were difficult to understand.
  • Figuring out how to make data readable and understandable.
  • Data from 1950 to today, 9000 days, creating a function to convert into a usable format(month, day and year).
  • Learning Dot format.
