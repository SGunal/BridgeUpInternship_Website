Our Mission/Goals

1. To make data science more inclusive

From web development to user experience design the technological world is not where it needs to be in terms of inclusiveness and diversity. 35.3 percent of female students take data science courses compared to 62.8 percant of male students. Also people of minority groups are the lowest percentage of students who enroll in data science courses. We hope by creating this website users who are underrepresneted in the field of data science can learn more about data science and see what data science can do for them. This will hopfully inspire individuals to study the field more extensively to close those gaps.

2. To make data science less intimidating

Contrary to popular belief data science is very simple. It's very much like how when we come across somthing new, we explore it. Data science is similar to that, but you are just doing that "exploring" in the computer. By using this website you will notice that data science can be applied to any thing that has data. Data science is a just a new way to learn about the world. It's more universal then people believe it to be. It's a new way to do something we have always done. It's a new way to explore the data around you.
