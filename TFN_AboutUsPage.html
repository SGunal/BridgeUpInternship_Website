Missions and Goals:

Make data science more inclusive

From web development to user experience design the tecnological world is not where it needs to be in terms of inclusiveness and diversity. 35.3% of female students take data science courses compared to 62.8% of male students. Also people of minority groups are the lowest percentage of students who enroll in data sceince courses. We hope by creating this website, users who are underrepresneted in the feild of data scince can learn more about data scinece and see what data science can do for them. This will hopfully inspire individual to study the field more extensivly close those gaps.

Make data science less intimidating

Countraty to pouplar belief data scinece is very simple. Very much like how when we come across somthing new we explore it. Data scinece is similar to that, nut you are just doing that "exploring" in the computer. By using this wedsite you will notice that data scinece can be applied to any thing that has data. Data scinece is a just a new way to learn about the world. Its more universal then people believe it to be. Its a new way to do something we have always done. It's a new way to explore the data around you.
